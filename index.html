02
Chapter Two

Infinite
Precision

Within the Calibre AV-7 beats 312 meticulously finished components. Each gear, spring, and jewel is assembled by hand — a ritual performed by the same maîtres horlogers who have dedicated their lives to the pursuit of mechanical perfection. Forty-one ruby jewels reduce friction across every critical bearing surface; the balance wheel oscillates at 28,800 vibrations per hour, dividing each second into eight equal heartbeats of pure mechanical logic.

Swiss Craftsmanship
03
Chapter Three

Born
from Fire

Forged at 1,400 degrees Celsius over twenty-four hours, our proprietary zirconia ceramic undergoes a molecular transformation — aluminium oxide crystals fusing into a lattice of uncompromising density. At 1,200 Vickers on the hardness scale, it surpasses steel ninefold. It does not tarnish, does not scratch, does not yield to time.

Ceramic Mastery
04
Chapter Four

72 Hours
of Power

The AV-7 movement's twin mainspring barrels operate in series — the first barrel absorbs the initial burst of winding energy while the second releases it with unwavering regularity across three full days. This dual-barrel architecture ensures constant torque delivery from full-wind to reserve, guaranteeing COSC-certified precision throughout.
